Attendee Journey

The Journey of Missed Opportunity

🔍

Discovery: The Event Echo Chamber

Platforms suggest events based on topic tags (#AI, #Marketing), creating self-reinforcing loops. They can't answer crucial questions:

"Which event will have the 3 people I need to meet to get my startup funded?"

"Where are practitioners discussing LangChain failure modes, not just hype?"

🧊

Pre-Event: The Social Cold Start

Between 'Register' and event day, there's a vacuum. Attendees enter as strangers, forcing every interaction to be a cold start. No way to see who's going, what questions they're pondering, or what micro-communities are forming.

👻

During Event: The Digital Bouncer

Platform's role is reduced to QR code scanning. Rich, real-time data is lost: applause patterns, coffee machine debates, unasked questions. The platform becomes invisible during the most valuable moments.

💨

Post-Event: The Great Evaporation

Massive intellectual, social, and inspirational energy dissipates instantly. Frantic LinkedIn exchanges, dead PDF slides, lost discussions. The platform acts as a container with a hole in the bottom.

Organizer Challenges

The Frustration of Wasted Potential

📣

The Empty Megaphone

Terrible at discovering and attracting the right NEW people. Blunt instruments: social posts, paid ads. No lookalike audiences based on deep interest graphs.

📊

The Vanity Dashboard

Drowning in shallow data. Can't answer: Which speaker generated most follow-on conversations? Who are the "hidden influencers"? What was the true "Return on Attention"?

🏢

The Logo-Industrial Complex

Sponsor model based on "exposure" - logos on screens, booths in halls. No way to demonstrate tangible ROI beyond badge scans, which are poor proxies for genuine interest.

🌪️

The Community Diaspora

Conversation immediately offloaded to Slack/Discord, fragmenting user experience and breaking contextual links. Platform becomes worst tool for community building.

Continuum: Knowledge Persistence & Community

Integrated Platform Feature

Feature Overview

Transforms events from point-in-time occurrences into persistent, living Knowledge Graphs. Captures intellectual output and makes it explorable, citable, and community-building.

Core Capabilities

📋
The Living Agenda

Each session becomes a container: transcript, AI summary, moment-by-moment engagement graph, Q&A threads.

💎
Knowledge Artifacts

Key insights "minted" by attendees, linked to source talks and speakers. Building blocks of community's shared brain.

🔗
Connection Provenance

"You and John were both engaged during Vector Databases session and asked about performance scaling." Maps connections via shared curiosity.

🌊
Post-Event Insight Streams

Shows how event ideas evolve, get referenced, connect to new topics. Continuous value delivery.

Business Impact

Perfect Fit

High-signal, content-heavy technical and scientific conferences (NeurIPS, Strange Loop, PyCon) where information longevity is paramount.

Revenue Model

Knowledge persistence features create long-term platform stickiness and enable premium community management subscriptions.

Business Model

B2B SaaS: freemium basic listings, paid tiers for knowledge capture + AI features, enterprise tier for anonymized knowledge graph access.
